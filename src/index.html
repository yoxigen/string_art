    String Art Studio

    Welcome to String Art Studio! With this app you can design and produce your own beautiful string art projects.

    How-to

    1. Choose a pattern
    2. Change options (like number of nails, shapes and colors)
    3. Download and print a map of nail positions
    4. Drive nails into a wood panel through the nails map
    5. Put strings to the nails as shown in the app's player. You can play this process in the app's player and pause, to make things clear.
    6. You're done!
